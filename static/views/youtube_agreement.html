Commission Determination:

Under the Influencer Program, you will be paid a Commission Fee for each Unique User that signs your card and validates his/her email on the WishYOO platform in accordance with the terms of this Agreement. Each of these Referred Users must meet the following criteria (the “Criteria”):

  • Each Referred Users must be registered as a unique user to the WishYOO Platform and must have a valid Wishyoo user account as describe herein.
  • A valid user account must have a unique email address. This email address cannot be part of those considered to be, known for or listed as “temporary” or “disposable” email addresses.
  • A valid Referred User must have ‘signed’ your YouTubeR Birthday card if they add any kind of message or signature to your card’s ‘Signature Board.’
  • Each unique Referred User can only sing ONCE on a Youtuber card for this event. Repeated participation will not be considered as a valid participation towards your Commission fees.
  • Commissions will be calculated off the total number of Referred Users who have signed your signature board by 11:59 p.m. (your local time) on the day of your birthday. If a Referred Users’s participation has to be removed at our sole discretion for any reason the User will no longer be considered to have ‘signed’ or participated on your card.

WishYOO will pay special attention to signatures and dedications that may be done by the same person in order to artificially enhance Commission fees for Influencers. If such cases are encountered by Wishyoo, we reserve the right to erased your card and cancel your participation as a result of this behavior. 

More Trees, Inc. reserves the right to suspend payment of Commission Fees at any time and indefinitely, if it suspects fraud or other improper activity or a potential breach of any of the terms in this Agreement by the Influencer.

If an Influencer has a question about a Commission Fee that has been cancelled or withheld, that Influencer has 30 days from the day the payment was due to contact More Trees, Inc. to discuss any matters related to the Commission Fee. Any changes to decisions about cancelled or withheld Commission Fees are strictly at WishYOO’s discretion.

 

Commission Fee Accrual and Payments:

Subject to the terms of this Agreement, we will pay a Commission Fee equal to the specified USD amount set forth here – USD$ 0.10 per Referred User.

The Influencer(s) with the highest ratio of Referred Users compared to the number of subscribers on the qualifying YouTube channel will be eligible for a one-time bonus of $25,000. In the event that multiple Influencers achieve the same results at the end of the event (September 30th 2018) on their Card, the bonus will be divided and distributed amongst those Influencers.

Commission Fees will be processed and paid out within 30 days of the closing of the Influencer’s YouTubeR Birthday Card (closing occurs automatically at 11:59 p.m. on the day of your birthday).

The winner of the $25,000 bonus will be determined at the end of the entire event (December 31st, 2018). Dispersal of the bonus will be completed within 60 days of the end of the event.

Commissions will accrue and only become payable once you (i) provide all relevant documentation and (ii) you have sent us an invoice regarding the amount agreed to be paid to you based on the activity of your Referred Users.
